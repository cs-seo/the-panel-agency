## What this changes about our strategy

### New high-priority pages to build

1. **`/learn/how-to-claim-a-google-knowledge-panel`** — covers "claim knowledge panel" 90/mo + "knowledge panel claim" 30/mo. The claim process is currently mentioned briefly in `/solutions/verification-badge` but doesn't deserve its own page. This *does*.
2. **`/learn/google-knowledge-panel-login-claim-guide`** — pivot the "login" search volume (2,400/mo combined!) into a guide that explains the *claim* process for owners. This is buyer-intent traffic mis-mapped to a "login" query. Massive opportunity.
3. **`/solutions/knowledge-panel-for-artists`** — close the 90/mo gap. Same template as `/solutions/knowledge-panel-for-authors` but for musicians, visual artists, and creators.
4. **`/learn/semantic-seo-vs-entity-seo`** — captures the 880/mo "semantic seo" search head term. Reframe semantic SEO as a sub-discipline of entity SEO.

### Pages to tighten with new keyword targeting

- `/learn/how-knowledge-panels-work` should integrate: `wikidata api`, `wikidata sparql`, `wikidata knowledge graph`, `google knowledge graph api`, `kgmid`, `entity optimization`.
- `/solutions/knowledge-panel-for-founders` and `for-authors` should both add "semantic seo" and "entity SEO" as supporting context.
- Homepage meta title currently says "Google Knowledge Panel Setup & Entity Verification" — strong but should be stress-tested by GSC after a few weeks.

### What we are NOT chasing

- **"knowledge panel" alone (5,400/mo)** — too contaminated by Ipsos. We compete by being unmistakably about *Google* Knowledge Panels everywhere on the site (we already are).
- **"knowledge panel survey/sign up"** — Ipsos users, no commercial overlap.
- **"helium seo", "kalicube pro"** — competitor brands. Useful intelligence, not targets.
